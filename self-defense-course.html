Self-Defense Course

Our self-defense course at DoBu Academy is designed to equip you with practical skills and techniques to protect yourself in real-world situations. This comprehensive program covers essential aspects of personal safety, including situational awareness, effective striking, escape maneuvers, and defense against common attacks.

Our experienced instructors provide hands-on training in a supportive and empowering environment, ensuring you gain confidence and competence. Whether you're a beginner or looking to enhance your existing skills, our self-defense course offers the tools you need to feel safer and more secure in your daily life.
